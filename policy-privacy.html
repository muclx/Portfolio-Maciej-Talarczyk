5. Two basic types of cookies are used within the website: "session" (session cookies) and "persistent" cookies. "Session" cookies are temporary files that are stored on the end-user's device until logging out, leaving the website, or turning off the software (web browser). "Persistent" cookies are stored on the end-user's device for the time specified in the parameters of the cookies or until they are deleted by the end-user.

Limitations on the use of cookies may affect some of the functionalities available on the website.
